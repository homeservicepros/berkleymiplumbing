Safe DIY Plumbing Projects

Some plumbing issues are perfectly safe for homeowners to tackle:

  • Unclogging simple sink or shower drains
  • Fixing running toilets
  • Replacing faucet washers
  • Installing new showerheads
  • Plunging toilet clogs

When to Call an Emergency Plumber

1. Burst Pipes

If you discover a burst pipe, immediately shut off your water main and contact our emergency burst pipe repair services. This situation can quickly cause extensive water damage, especially in areas like Berkley South.

2. Sewer Line Backups

Sewage backing up into your home requires immediate professional attention. Our hydro jetting drain cleaning services can quickly resolve these dangerous situations.

3. Hidden Leaks

If you notice unusually high water bills or hear running water when no fixtures are in use, you may need our slab leak detection repair services.

Warning Signs That Require Professional Attention

  • Multiple clogged drains
  • Water pressure changes throughout your home
  • Unusual sounds from your plumbing system
  • Water discoloration
  • Persistent sewer odors

DIY Safety Tips

If you decide to tackle a DIY plumbing project, remember:

  • Always know where your main water shut-off valve is located
  • Use proper tools and safety equipment
  • Never mix chemical drain cleaners
  • Document your steps when disassembling fixtures
