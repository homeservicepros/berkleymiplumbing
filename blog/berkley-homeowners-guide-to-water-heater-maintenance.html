Essential Water Heater Maintenance Tasks

Monthly Maintenance Checklist

  • Check for visible leaks around the unit
  • Listen for unusual sounds
  • Test the pressure relief valve
  • Inspect the area around your water heater for moisture

Annual Maintenance Requirements

  • Flush the tank to remove sediment buildup
  • Check the anode rod
  • Inspect electrical connections (for electric heaters)
  • Test water temperature settings

Signs Your Water Heater Needs Professional Attention

While regular maintenance helps, some issues require professional intervention. If you notice any of these signs, it's time to contact our Water Heater Repair Berkley Michigan experts:

  • Rusty or discolored water
  • Inconsistent water temperature
  • Strange noises from the tank
  • Visible corrosion or leaks
  • Age exceeding 10-12 years
